Cookie Policy

Last updated on: 15 December 2023

Hello! Welcome to the Navon website, navonholdings.com. This website, along with all related services, is operated by us – Navon. Our company is based in Romania, and you can reach us at contact@navonjobs.com.

So, let's talk about cookies. No, not the chocolate chip kind. These are digital cookies and similar technologies, like tags, web beacons, pixels, and, for mobile apps, software development kits or SDKs, that enhance your experience on our Website and help us recommend content you might find interesting. When we talk about cookies in this policy, we're referring to all of these technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites to make your browsing experience more efficient. We use various types of cookies and similar technologies, such as web beacons and pixels, to provide, secure, and improve our services. These technologies help us understand how you interact with our website and ensure a seamless experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

When you visit our Website and consent to cookies, these are the types of cookies that may be downloaded to your device:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website's basic functionality and required for the administration and operation of the Website and app.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences to provide a personalized experience, showing you content that may be relevant to you.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us analyze website usage for improvements, counting visitors, session duration, and navigation patterns.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and tracking across websites, remembering your visit, the pages you've viewed, and the links you've followed to other websites.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You’re in control of your cookies. You can adjust your preferences through the cookie banner on our website or through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our site.

Until you make up your mind, we'll only use the strictly necessary cookies.

If you want to adjust your cookie settings, you can do so by using the options on the banner that appears on our Website or through your browser settings.

For more detailed guidance on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org or allaboutcookies.org.

Please note, if you decide to go cookie-free (including the strictly necessary ones), some features of our Website may not be accessible.
